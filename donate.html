Donate

Support LiquidBounce's development by donating.

Donate now

We are thankful for every donation.

LiquidBounce is completely free. Nevertheless, we are happy about any voluntary support we can receive. In return for your donation you will receive an in-game cape that can be seen by all other LiquidBounce users. LiquidBounce is also able to automatically move your cape to the account you are currently logged into. So it doesn't matter if you play on your main account or not. Please note that you will have to donate at least $4 if you want to receive a cape!

PayPal is the preferred payment method for donations, but we also accept various crypto currencies.

After your donation you will receive a so-called transfer code (we will also send you this code again by email). This is the so to speak password to your cape. You need it to move your cape to another account and to switch it on or off. Don't share your code with anyone and don't lose it!

PayPal

If you pay via PayPal, the entire payment process is automated. You will receive your cape and a confirmation email immediately after your payment.

Just enter the Minecraft username you want to link your cape with in the form below and then make your payment. In the future you will be able to move the cape to other accounts. Therefore this is not a final decision. No worries.

Crypto Currencies

Besides PayPal we also accept the crypto currencies listed below. However, all payments here must be manually verified by us. Therefore it may take a few days until you receive your cape. Please make sure to send us an email after you have made your payment, otherwise we might not notice it. Our email address is support@liquidbounce.net. We will get back to you as soon as possible.

Forgot Transfer Code

If you lost your transfer code, you can recover it here.

To restore your transfer code you have several options available. So please consider which one is most suitable for your purposes.

  • I have access to the E-Mail address linked to the PayPal account I made my donation from and have donated after November 2, 2019.

    After receiving a donation, we automatically send a confirmation mail to the email address linked to the PayPal account used. Among other details, this email contains your transfer code. So before considering one of the other options, first search your mailbox for it. All confirmation emails we send have the title 'Thanks for your donation!' and were sent from the email account 'support@liquidbounce.net'.

  • I have access to the Minecraft account to which my cape is currently linked.

    As long as you have access to the account your cape is currently linked to, a recovery can be performed using Minecraft oAuth. To do this, log in to the aforementioned account, start Minecraft 1.8.9 and join the server at srv.mc-oauth.net:25565. You should immediately get kicked after joining. You will be given a code which you must enter in the text field below. After you have done this, submit the form. If there is a donator cape on the account, you will be provided with your transfer code here.

  • I have access to the PayPal account from which I made my donation and have donated after November 2, 2019.

    If you have access to the PayPal account from which you made your donation, a fairly simple recovery option is possible as well. You only need the transaction code of your payment. You can find this code in the transaction details of your donation on PayPal's website. Copy the transaction code into the text field below and submit the form. If there is a donation with the given transaction code in our system, you will get your transfer code restored here.

  • None of the above options are applicable to me.

    If none of the above options are suitable for you, you will have to contact us directly. Please write an e-mail to support@liquidbounce.net describing the problem you are facing. Then we can discuss which options are available at this point.
